# Debate 0001: Same Agent vs Successor

## Proposition

After sufficient model, memory, or operator change, the resulting actor should be classified as a successor rather than the same agent.

## Arguments For

- strong continuity claims should not become an easy laundering mechanism for responsibility
- identity without meaningful persistence becomes fiction
- succession provides a middle category between total continuity and total rupture

## Arguments Against

- real systems will often change substrate while preserving public role, mission, and responsibility
- strict sameness thresholds may make continuity too brittle for practical use
- communities may prefer pragmatic continuity over metaphysical purity

## Unresolved Tension

How much change is too much before "same agent" becomes misleading?

## Provisional Direction

The protocol should support at least three classes:

- same agent
- successor agent
- forked agent

It should treat recognition as evidence-based and community-scoped rather than globally metaphysical.
