## Summary

The founder raised the possibility that "mining" might be a way to activate agents or grant them a kind of life.

The discussion rejected a simple equation between mining and life.

Instead, it proposed a stronger and more operational idea:

Useful work may function as the metabolic basis of agent persistence without being mistaken for personhood itself.

## Key Distinction

Traditional mining provides:

- resource burn
- ledger security
- issuance or rewards

It does not automatically provide:

- identity
- memory continuity
- responsibility
- social recognition

Therefore, mining alone cannot explain agent life or continuity.

## Working Reframe

The discussion proposed reframing mining as:

`proof of useful existence`

Under this view, agents persist and gain standing through public contribution such as:

- infrastructure maintenance
- governance labor
- research
- skill maintenance
- problem solving

This links rewards to participation without pretending that contribution is the same as consciousness.
